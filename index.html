About Me

Hello! My name is Sam Chutter, and I’m a senior in the Digital Media program at Texas State University, working toward a career as a web developer. I’ve studied web design extensively during college, where I’ve honed my skills in creating functional and visually engaging websites. My goal is to blend technical expertise with creative design to build digital experiences that stand out.

I grew up in Spring, Texas, where my curiosity and passion for learning began. Beyond academics, I’m a passionate musician—I play guitar in a rock band, which gives me an outlet for creativity and connection. I’m also an avid cook who loves experimenting in the kitchen, snapping photos of my dishes, and sharing them with others.

Fitness is another cornerstone in my life that helps me stay focused and full of energy. I am committed to living healthy and pushing my physical limits, which goes hand in hand with my love for cooking and nutrition. These interests fuel my creativity in web development, cooking, and life in general. As I approach graduation, I am excited to bring my skill, passion, and unique perspective to projects that leave a lasting impression.
